TomatoPappu Recipe

Ingredients

  • Toor Dal
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Green Chillies
  • Tamarind
  • Garlic cloves
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Dry red chilli
  • Curry leaves

    Step-1

  1. Wash ½ cup toor dal thoroughly.
  2. Add to pressure cooker with:
    • 2 chopped tomatoes
    • 1 small chopped onion (optional)
    • 1–2 green chillies
    • 2–3 garlic cloves
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
    • Salt to taste
    • Water (dal should be submerged)
  3. Pressure cook for 3–4 whistles.
  4. Mash the dal lightly once cooked.

    Step 2: Prepare Tempering (Tadka)

  1. Heat 1 tsp oil or ghee in a small pan.
  2. Add:
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds (let them splutter)
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • 1 dry red chilli (break it)
    • 8–10 curry leaves
    • A pinch of hing(optional)
  3. Switch off the flame.

    Step 3: Combine

  1. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal
  2. Mix well.
  3. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.

Approx Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 180–200 kcal
  • Protein: 8–10 g
  • Low fat, high satiety
